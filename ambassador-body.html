Yopisode Ambassador Program

About Yopisode Ambassador Program

The Yopisode Amabassador Program is a leadership community development program for high school and university students to help their student community make learning more fun by organizig video watching events (YopEvents) to consume curiculla educational video in groups.


Who can join?

Students in high schools and universities who love TV, videos and love to organize people.


Why join?

  • Help your school community by organizing fun ways to learn with video curricula content.
  • Add volunteering, leadership and community development expereince to your CV.
  • Build a fun community and have fun.
  • Exclusive rights to organize YopEvents in connection with school
  • Stand the chance to become the Hero Ambassador for your area, and win a brand new chromebook, chromecast or an android phone.


Responsibilities of an Ambassador

  • Organize curricula YopEvents at least once in 3 months
  • Communicate with your school administration about each YopEvent
  • Build a team to support you
  • Promote the organzing of other fun YopEvents out of school like sports, TV series etc.
  • Report progress of YopEvent activities to Community Manager


Resources to Organize

As a Yopisode Ambassador, you are responsible for creating events and making sure that it they are fun and successful. You need some resources to do that. You have to identify all needed resources and communicate with different stakeholders to help you acquire these resources. In any doubt contact your community manager.
Key resources usually include:

  • Spot for YopEvent. (Be in good communication with school authorities. Usually the school would provide this).
  • Projector set
  • Video content
  • Swag and marketing material
  • Snacks


How to Join

To apply to become a Yopisode ambassador, email the following information to the Community Manager. community[at]yopisode.com

  • Full Names
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • School
  • Student ID
  • Name of a Staffer in your school we can contact
  • City
  • Country
  • How did you learn about Yopisode Ambassador Program
  • Why do you want to become a Yopisode Ambassador
  • What do you expect from Yopisode
