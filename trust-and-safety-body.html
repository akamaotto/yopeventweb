Trust & Safety

We are free because we trust that we can be safe with the people around us and with our environment. To keep the TV Community strong and healthy, we encourage you to try the following Trust & Safety tips


Trust & Safety Tips

  • Do not accept total strangers into your home.
  • Do not leave sharp objects around. People get jumpy and uncareful when excited.
  • Do not invite people into an unsafe place.
  • Always maintain your normal home or business policy as you would operate without the yopevent.
  • Keep a first aid kit
  • Let everyone know of various exit doors in case of anything.


We trust that you will do your best to keep you and your guests safe all the time
