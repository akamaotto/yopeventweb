How It Works

1. Find a yopevent

Whether you are pulled in from a social network or a search engine, or you came through directly through our app or website, just use our search, geolocation or recommendation tools to find the best yopevents for you. Share other great yopevents on your social networks. You might just giving a fun opportunity to a friend by doing that.


2. Ask to join

Once you find the yopevent you want to join, click/ touch the 'Join' button to indicate your interest in attending an event. For paid events (coming soon), click the 'Buy Ticket' button. Other people who have joined the event will be notified that you joined the event.

For paid event, choose your payment method and make the payment to buy your tickets. You can also message the organizer to find out more if you want.

Try to share the yopevent on your social network and email. Yopevents without friends could be damn boring.


3. Attend the yopevent

After you must have made sure that you are a valid guest to a yopevent, sorted out the address, and confirmed with your host (The Organizer), suit up with your fun dress and go yop the event with friends.
