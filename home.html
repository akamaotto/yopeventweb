Let's Watch TV Together

Invite your friends and watch a TV program together. Have fun!


How it works

How To Organize

create a yopevent

Think of a nice TV program and create a yopevent for you and your friends to watch TV together.

invite friends

Share your yopevent on Facebook, Twitter, email and other social networs to invite friends.

have fun

Setup your TV. Prepare the spot. Get some snacks & Have some fun. Your friends will love you.

Why Organize

Become an agent of fun and happiness. It's boring watching TV alone for you and for your friends. Create a yopevent and kick boredom away.

Why Attend

While you sit alone watching TV, your friends are watching TV together. Stop being bored, find out where they are, go meet them and have fun!
