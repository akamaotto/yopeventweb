About Yopisode

We are a community of tv geeks and we love coffee.


We build fun tools every day to help people catch fun and enjoy stories together


We respect everyone's opinions and everyone is expected to contribute.


We code, we build communities and we watch TV.


We always welcome cheerful and talented persons who want to contribute in helping the TV watching/ Yopisode Community more fun.


You are welcomed to use Yopisode to start your career or to use it as a next step in your career. You want a full-time job, you want to intern, you want to volunteer or you want to contribute in some other special way. Just contact us.


We need coders, designers, lean startup followers, fund-raisers, community managers, business development managers and lots more. Just contact us if you think we need you.
